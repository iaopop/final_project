Musical «Julia»

Almaty Theatre

Since 26 December

Further

"Julia" is a musical based on the best hits of the legendary band A'Studio. The story is based on a story that speaks a language that anyone in the world understands - the language of love and music. The theme song of the musical will immerse itself in memories of the past, because it is so touching and beautiful, will take us into the events of our time, and will also awaken in everyone the desire and ability to believe in true sincere feelings. "Julia" is a limitless source of emotions: laughter through tears, nostalgia, inspiration and desire to create and do things. Everything happens in the name of love: the audience will be able to see this again, having lived the story with the heroes.

Do not miss the chance to become a spectator of the Kazakh musical, which will go down in history!
